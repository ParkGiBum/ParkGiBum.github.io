Research

My research focuses on LLM serving systems and mobile–cloud collaborative inference, with an emphasis on hardware-aware, efficient LLM inference. I am interested in redesigning where and how computation is split between edge devices and servers so that large models can be served with low latency without sacrificing accuracy.
